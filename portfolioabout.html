About Me

Daisy Rono is a web developer, web designer and professional photographer with experience creating mobile web development solutions and responsive website designs, to building custom e-commerce and intranet experiences using the latest and proven web technologies. My educational background in Computer Science and part-time roles at the beginning of my career inform my mindful but competitive approach. I am fueled by my passion for understanding inclusivity of every product I design in meeting every cultural and behavioural needs of the clients. I consider myself a 'forever student' eager to both build on my academic foundation in technological tools and stay in tune with the ever evolving technologies through continued research.

My hunger to turn knowledge and latest technological information into action has contributed to my most recent success at ABC Company where I have led International, award-winning campaigns for animal and environmental preservation websites. Meanwhile, I have vastly improved my team's productivity by implementing strategic project management methods and ensuring a work-life balance for my department.

The key to success is paying attention to the clients needs and making appealing designs which make the usability of the device easier for users. Please see my portfolio and resume to reach out to me.
